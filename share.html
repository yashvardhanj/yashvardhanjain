ShARE

I am an associate member at ShARE, Technology Students Gymkhana, IIT Kharagpur.

OUR PURPOSE We believe that our society needs a new economic paradigm – which is more balanced and combines economic performance and societal progress.

We call this system: Do Well Do Good.

It is a system where : Companies both serve the shareholders and the society, Individuals have a thriving career while contributing to the society, Governments and NGOs are more effective at serving the society. While this shift has begun, it is still too slow and needs to be accelerated to solve major societal issues such as inequalities, climate and biodiversity.

WHAT WE DO ShARE's purpose is to contribute to create a “Do Well Do Good” economy through two interlinked activities. EDUCATION - Growing a new generation of leaders who will Do Well and Do Good. CONSULTING - Advising companies, institutions and governments to Do Well and Do Good.
